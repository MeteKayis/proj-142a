AI PING PONG






Instructions

  1. First Keep your Device Screen Straight
  2. Move yourself about 3-4 ft away from the Screen
  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and decending matter.
    A red dot will appear
  4. Now press play, keeping the 3-4 ft distance from the Screen
  5. Red = Yours
  6. The Paddle will follow your wrist
  7. 3-4 ft is the greatest distance for the AI to detect your Wrist
